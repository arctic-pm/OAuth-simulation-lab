// KEY TAKEAWAYS FROM THIS LAB
01
OAuth tokens are as sensitive as passwords. Anyone holding a valid token can impersonate you silently.
02
Supply chain attacks target the weakest third-party app you trust — not you directly. Your token is only as safe as the app storing it.
03
Overpermissioned scopes dramatically increase the blast radius of a stolen token. Always grant the minimum required scope.
04
Token revocation is an effective and immediate countermeasure. Tokens have a lifespan — revoking them cuts off attacker access instantly.
05
Audit connected apps regularly at myaccount.google.com/permissions and revoke anything you no longer actively use.

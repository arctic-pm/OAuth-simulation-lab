// 06 — DEFENSIVE COUNTERMEASURES
COUNTERMEASURE 01
Revoke the Token
Call the identity provider's revocation endpoint immediately. The token becomes invalid and cannot be replayed.
COUNTERMEASURE 02
Verify with 401
Attempt to use the revoked token. A 401 Unauthorized confirms the token is dead and the attacker is locked out.
COUNTERMEASURE 03
Audit All OAuth Apps
Regularly review and revoke third-party app permissions at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
🔐 REVOKE TOKEN NOW

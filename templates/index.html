OAUTH 2.0 ATTACK
SIMULATION LAB

Understand OAuth token exposure through hands-on demonstration

// ABOUT THIS LAB

OAuth supply chain attacks are among the most common and damaging real-world breach patterns. Attackers don't always target you directly — they target a trusted third-party application you've granted access to, steal the stored OAuth tokens, and use them to impersonate you silently. This lab recreates that mechanism with your full knowledge and consent.

→

→
